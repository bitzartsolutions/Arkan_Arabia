FAQ

Common Questions

What is your average response time for inquiries? expand_more
Our dedicated team responds within 45 minutes during business hours (Sun–Thu, 08:00–18:00 AST). For urgent matters, our 24/7 emergency line ensures immediate assistance.
Can I get a quote without providing detailed cargo information? expand_more
Yes, you can submit a preliminary inquiry with basic details. Our team will contact you to gather the necessary specifications and provide a tailored quote within 2 business hours.
Do you provide support in Arabic? expand_more
Absolutely. All our GCC offices have fully bilingual teams (Arabic and English). We also provide support in Urdu and Hindi for regional operations.
How do I track my existing shipment? expand_more
Existing clients can track shipments through the Arkan Digital Portal. New to tracking? Contact our support team and we'll set up your account and provide your tracking credentials.
