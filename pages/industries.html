Industry FAQs

How does Arkan manage cross-border customs for construction machinery? expand_more
We maintain a dedicated customs brokerage team specifically for project cargo. They handle ATA carnets, temporary import permits, and duty exemptions to ensure heavy machinery moves through borders without administrative delays.
Do you provide temperature-controlled storage for pharmaceuticals? expand_more
Yes, our warehousing facilities include specialized GWP-certified cold storage areas with real-time temperature logging and 24/7 alert systems to maintain the cold chain integrity of sensitive medical cargo.
What is Arkan's experience with government and defense logistics? expand_more
Arkan has a proven track record of handling high-security shipments for government agencies. We utilize secure terminals, vetted staff, and encrypted tracking protocols for sensitive infrastructure projects.
How do you support e-commerce last-mile delivery across GCC? expand_more
We operate dedicated urban distribution hubs in major GCC cities, enabling same-day and next-day delivery options. Our returns management process reduces cost and complexity for online retailers.
